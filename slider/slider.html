To make this slider I made a container for the content. I find positioning a div both vertically and horizontally center is easiest with jquery, so I wrote a couple lines to do that. Next I made 4 content containers and had them hide offscreen to the right based on the width of the container. When a button is clicked it takes the active class and slides it to the left, then once its off screen it instantly positions it off to the right, ready to be slid again. The new active class slides in from the right.
